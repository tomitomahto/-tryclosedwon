Our Story

Built by someone who had to figure it out without a roadmap.

I moved to Canada from Nigeria at 17 with almost nothing. No money. No network. No one to call and ask what to do next.

My first job after university, the company went bankrupt. So I did what anyone would do. I Googled which jobs make good money. Sales came up. I had no idea what I was doing. I just knew I could talk to people and I was hungry enough to figure out the rest.

That stumble eventually led to a career that took me from cold calling strangers to leading a new business sales team at Google Canada, building a beauty brand that landed in the New York Times, and founding a coaching platform for ambitious sales professionals.

Sales gave me everything. But the path was harder than it needed to be. Not because the work was hard, but because the information and tools that should have existed simply did not.

I built Closed Won for everyone who is building a sales career without a roadmap. The ones figuring it out at midnight. The ones who do not have the warm introductions or the right university or the network. The ones who are just hungry enough to figure out the rest.
